How does this work?

- We use a LMM (kinda like an LLM) to rate your uploaded photo on different factors.


Do you store my photos?

- No. We store all images in memory until they are uploaded, and thereafter are no longer stored. We store ratings, which contain no personal info about you.


How can I share my rating with a friend or someone else?

- It's as simple as copying and pasting the link, or clicking the share icon in your browser.
